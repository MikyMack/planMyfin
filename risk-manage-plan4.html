    Key Principles of Effective Risk Management

    Prioritize Pure Protection

    Focus on term insurance for life cover‚Äîpure protection without investment mixing. It's affordable and provides maximum coverage.

    Adequate Coverage is Essential

    Under-insurance is as risky as no insurance. Ensure coverage matches your actual financial responsibilities and future needs.

    Start Early, Pay Less

    Insurance premiums are age-based. Starting early means lower premiums and longer protection period for your family.

    Regular Reviews Matter

    Life changes‚Äîmarriage, children, promotions, loans. Review and update your coverage as your life evolves.

    Don't Mix Insurance & Investment

    Traditional insurance-cum-investment plans often provide inadequate coverage. Keep them separate for better results.

    Disclosure is Critical

    Always provide accurate information. Non-disclosure can lead to claim rejection when your family needs it most.
