Financial Wellness Educational programs

Equip your students with industry-ready financial planning skills through our comprehensive certificate programs and interactive workshops.

Empowering the Next Generation of Financial Professionals

In today's complex financial landscape, essential money management skills are no longer optional—they're critical life competencies. Our comprehensive financial wellness programs equip MBA students and business graduates with practical, real-world financial planning expertise that prepares them for both professional success and personal financial well-being.

Certificate Course in Financial Planning

Why This Program Matters

Financial decision-making has become increasingly complex due to evolving products, dynamic economic conditions, and the growing need for individuals to take responsibility for their financial well-being. This 30-hour certificate course transforms theoretical knowledge into practical competency—equipping participants with the clarity, skills, and confidence needed to manage their own finances and advise others effectively.

Program Approach

Designed as a practical, personalized coaching experience, this program delivers a complete mindset upgrade. Participants gain fundamental financial literacy, hands-on planning tools, and actionable strategies to achieve financial goals and build lifelong wealth-building habits. The curriculum aligns with institutional missions to enhance employability, develop practical competencies, and support holistic student development.

Learning Outcomes

Graduates will master the foundations of personal finance, prepare detailed financial plans, assess risks and protection needs, build investment strategies, analyze debt management options, understand taxation basics, and use professional financial planning tools—all while enhancing their employability with industry-aligned insights.

Our Workshop & Training Offerings

We offer flexible, engaging sessions designed to introduce students to essential financial wellness concepts. From one-time introductory workshops to comprehensive certification programs.

INTRODUCTORY

Financial Wellness Orientation Session

A 2-hour introductory session covering the fundamentals of personal finance, budgeting, and financial goal setting—perfect for freshers and early-stage students.

HALF-DAY WORKSHOP

Money Management Essentials

A 4-hour interactive workshop on income management, expense tracking, emergency funds, and basic investment concepts with hands-on exercises.

FULL-DAY WORKSHOP

Financial Planning Fundamentals

An intensive 6-8 hour session covering budgeting, savings strategies, investment basics, debt management, and creating a personal financial plan.

WEEKEND BOOTCAMP

Investment & Wealth Building Bootcamp

A comprehensive 2-day program diving deep into investment instruments, risk profiling, asset allocation, and portfolio management strategies.

SPECIALIZED

Career Starters Financial Toolkit

Tailored for graduating students—covering salary negotiation, tax planning, loan management, credit building, and financial goal setting for early career professionals.

CERTIFICATION PROGRAM

30-Hour Certificate Course

A complete certification program covering 10 comprehensive modules from basic budgeting to advanced financial planning—ideal for semester-long integration or intensive training.

All programs can be customized to your institution's specific needs, student level, and time constraints.
