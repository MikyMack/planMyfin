      Frequently Asked Questions

      • Who can benefit from the Financial Wellness Programme?
        This programme is designed for individuals at any stage of their financial journey—whether you're just starting your career, planning for major life goals, approaching retirement, or running a business. Organizations can also implement this for their employees to improve productivity and reduce financial stress.
      • What makes this programme different from regular financial advice?
        Unlike product-centric financial advice, our programme focuses on empowering YOU with the skills to manage your own financial life. We provide objective, trustworthy resources that aren't driven by product sales. You'll learn to craft your own financial plan rather than just following recommendations.
      • How long does the programme take?
        The programme is customized based on your needs. It can range from intensive one-to-one consultations over a few sessions to ongoing coaching relationships. For organizations, we offer flexible formats including workshops, seminars, and comprehensive employee wellness plans.
      • Do I need to have financial knowledge before starting?
        No prior financial knowledge is required. The programme is designed to meet you where you are—whether you're a complete beginner or have some financial experience. We build your knowledge systematically, ensuring you understand each concept before moving forward.
