      Key Components of Cashflow Analysis

      1
      Income Streams

      Tracking all sources of income including salary, business income, rental income, and passive income streams

      2
      Fixed Expenses

      Regular, predictable costs like rent/EMI, insurance premiums, subscriptions, and loan payments

      3
      Variable Expenses

      Fluctuating costs such as groceries, utilities, entertainment, and discretionary spending

      4
      Savings & Investments

      Monthly contributions to emergency funds, retirement accounts, and investment portfolios

      5
      Net Cashflow

      The difference between total income and total expenses‚Äîyour financial surplus or deficit
